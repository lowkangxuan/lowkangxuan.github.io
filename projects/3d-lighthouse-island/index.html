Island Terrain

When creating the overall terrain for the island, I decided to sculpt the terrain itself in Unity using the Terrain Tool and texture it using Vertex Colors.

Sculpting

As mentioned above, the terrain itself is sculpted in Unity itself. After sculpting the island, I exported the mesh to ZBrush where I retopologized it to ensure the individual faces are proportional.

I then exported the retopologized mesh into Maya where I spent a couple of hours unwrapping the UVs just so that textures applied to the mesh would not look distorted.

Lighthouse

The lighthouse itself was modeled normally with multiple references to real-life lighthouse I've managed to find online.

I then added a Spotlight in Unity which rotates in a clockwise manner during runtime. This small little touch adds on to the overall experience where it would show the player that the lighthouse itself is still functioning and working well.
